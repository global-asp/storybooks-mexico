Veja os animais.

Mira a los animales.

Look at the animals.

A vaca diz, “muuu.”

La vaca dice, “Muu.”

The cow says, “Moo.”

A cabra diz, “mé, mé.”

La cabra dice, “Be, be.”

The goat says, “Meh, meh.”

O cavalo diz. “iihhh.”

El caballo dice, “Hin.”

The horse says, “Neigh.”

O porco diz, “ronc, ronc.”

El cerdo dice, “Oink-oink.”

The pig says, “Grunt.”

A galinha diz, “cocoricó.”

La gallina dice, “Cló-cló.”

The chicken says, “Cluck.”

O cachorro diz, “au, au.”

El perro dice, “Guau.”

The dog says, “Woof.”

O fazendeiro diz, “xiu!”

El granjero dice, “¡Chsss!”

The farmer says, “Shhh!”

Ilustraciones: Sandy Campbell
Traducción: Priscilla Freitas de Oliveira
Lectura en voz alta: Alfredo Ferreira
