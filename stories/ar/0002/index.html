Sandy Campbell

árabe

Nivel 1

اُنْظُرْ إِلَى الحَيَوَانَاتِ.

Mira a los animales.

Look at the animals.

البَقَرَةُ تَخُورُ.

La vaca dice, "Muu."

The cow says, "Moo."

الجَدْيُ يَقولُ “مييه… ميه”.

La cabra dice, "Be, be."

The goat says, "Meh, meh."

الحِصَانُ يَصْهَلُ.

El caballo dice, "Hin."

The horse says, "Neigh."

الخِنْزِيرُ يَنْخِرُ.

El cerdo dice, "Oink-oink."

The pig says, "Grunt."

الدَّجَاجَةُ تُقَرْقِرُ.

La gallina dice, "Cló-cló."

The chicken says, "Cluck."

الكَلْبُ يَنبَحُ.

El perro dice, "Guau."

The dog says, "Woof."

المُزَارِعُ يَقُولُ “شششش”.

El granjero dice, "¡Chsss!"

The farmer says, "Shhh!"
