看看农场里的动物。

Mira a los animales.

Look at the animals.

母牛“哞哞”叫。

La vaca dice, "Muu."

The cow says, "Moo."

山羊“咩咩”叫。

La cabra dice, "Be, be."

The goat says, "Meh, meh."

老马“嘶嘶”叫。

El caballo dice, "Hin."

The horse says, "Neigh."

小猪“哼哼”叫。

El cerdo dice, "Oink-oink."

The pig says, "Grunt."

母鸡“咯咯”叫。

La gallina dice, "Cló-cló."

The chicken says, "Cluck."

小狗“汪汪”叫。

El perro dice, "Guau."

The dog says, "Woof."

农夫就说“嘘嘘”。

El granjero dice, "¡Chsss!"

The farmer says, "Shhh!"
